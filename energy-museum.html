About

An interactive museum exploring energy's evolution - past, present, and future. Regional winner at AIS Design Olympiad 6, featuring transparent design and sustainable innovation.

Key Features

Zones

  • Historical, Contemporary, and Future Energy zones
  • Immersive storytelling experience

Interactive Elements

  • Touch-screens, AR/VR stations, digital displays
  • Real-time energy data visualization

Exhibition Space

  • Energy evolution timeline displays
  • Modular exhibition system

Sustainability

  • Solar integration and rainwater harvesting
  • Smart glazing and eco-materials

Facilities

  • Research lab and innovation center
  • Auditorium and workshop spaces
  • Accessible design with multi-language support
